Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 03, 2024

**Anbu Transport Service Private Limited** (operating as **TickMyBus**) ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share personal information when you use our services, including visiting our website **https://tickmybus.com/**, or any service that links to this Privacy Policy. By accessing or using our services, you agree to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you disagree with any part of this policy, please do not use our services.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us and information automatically when you interact with our services.

Personal Information Provided by You

This may include:

  • Names
  • Phone numbers
  • Email addresses
  • Mailing addresses
  • Usernames and passwords
  • Billing addresses and payment information (handled securely by our payment processor Razorpay)

Information Collected Automatically

We automatically collect:

  • Log and Usage Data: including IP address, browser type, and interactions with our services.
  • Device Data: such as device characteristics, operating system, and location data (if permitted).

How We Use Your Information

We process your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and improve our ticket booking services.
  • To process payments and manage transactions.
  • To communicate with you regarding account status, policy updates, or service changes.
  • To deliver marketing communications and personalized ads (you can opt out at any time).
  • To ensure security and fraud prevention.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Sharing Your Information

We may share your information in specific circumstances:

  • With third-party service providers (e.g., payment processors like Razorpay).
  • In the event of business transfers (mergers or acquisitions).
  • For legal compliance or protection from fraud or unauthorized activities.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and gather analytics about site usage. You can control cookie settings in your browser, but disabling cookies may impact your experience with our services.

Social Logins

If you choose to register or log in using your social media accounts (e.g., Facebook, X), we will collect certain profile information as provided by the social media platform.

Data Retention

We retain personal information as long as needed to provide our services and for legitimate business purposes. When no longer needed, we will delete or anonymize your data.

Security Measures

We have implemented appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no online system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the security of your data.

Minors

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us for its removal.

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Correct or update your personal information.
  • Delete your account at any time.
  • Withdraw consent where we rely on it for processing your data.

To exercise your rights, contact us at info@tickmybus.com
