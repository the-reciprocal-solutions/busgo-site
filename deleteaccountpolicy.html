Account Deletion Policy

Last updated September 03, 2024

  1. Overview
    2.    This policy outlines the process for deleting your account on Tickmybus. You can delete your account through both our app and website by accessing the account settings in    your profile.
  2. How to Delete Your Account
    3.    Via App:
    • Open the Tickmybus app and log in to your account.
    • Navigate to the profile or account settings section.
    • Select the "Delete Account" option.
    • Follow the prompts to confirm the deletion.
      • Via Website:
    • Log in to your account on the Tickmybus website.
    • Go to your profile settings.
    • Click on the "Delete Account" link.
    • Confirm your decision by following the instructions provided.
  3. What Happens After Deletion
    4.    Upon successful deletion of your account:
       - Your account and all associated data will be permanently removed from our active databases.
       - Some information may be retained in our backup archives to comply with legal requirements, prevent fraud, troubleshoot issues, or enforce our terms of service.
  4. Effects of Deletion
    5.    - Irreversible Action: Account deletion is irreversible. Once completed, you will not be able to recover any inf ormation or data previously associated with your account.
       - Loss of Access: You will no longer have access to your account or any associated services and data.
       - Irreversible Action: Account deletion is irreversible. Once completed, you will not be able to recover any information or data previously associated with your account.
  5. Questions or Assistance
    6.    If you have any questions or need assistance with account deletion, please contact our support team at [info@tickmybus.com](mailto:info@tickmybus.com).
  6. Updates to This Policy
